# Idea 05: Validity of Generalization Across Worlds

## Status
Non-normative research idea.

## Motivation
Generalization is frequently claimed across datasets or domains
without justification that the underlying worlds are compatible.

## WorldSeed Dependency
Requires:
- world identity declarations
- explicit scope of claims
